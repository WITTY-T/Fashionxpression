FAQ

Common Questions

How accurate are your measurements?
Our in-studio measurements achieve a 99.5% accuracy rate. For virtual measurements, our guided video process with detailed body diagram typically achieves 95% accuracy on the first fitting, with minor adjustments made during the fitting session.
How long does the bespoke process take?
Most bespoke garments are completed within 2-4 weeks from consultation to final delivery. Wedding and bridal pieces typically require 6-8 weeks. Rush orders are available for an additional fee — contact us for details.
Can I reschedule my virtual session?
Yes, you can reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment at no charge. Rescheduling within 24 hours may incur a small fee. Simply reply to your booking confirmation email or contact us via WhatsApp.
What if my fabric choice is out of stock?
If your preferred fabric is unavailable, we'll immediately notify you with three alternatives: (1) a similar fabric in stock, (2) a custom sourcing option with timeline, or (3) a refund for the fabric deposit. We never substitute without your explicit approval.
Do you offer alterations on non-bespoke items?
Yes! We offer alterations on ready-to-wear pieces purchased from our collection and on external garments. Basic alterations (hemming, taking in) start at ₦5,000. Complex alterations are quoted after assessment.
What is your satisfaction guarantee?
We stand behind every bespoke garment. If you're not completely satisfied after your final fitting, we'll make unlimited adjustments free of charge within 30 days of delivery. Your perfect fit is our promise.
