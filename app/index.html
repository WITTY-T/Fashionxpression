The Foundation

Our Fabric Library

Drag to explore our curated collection of authentic African textiles

Ankara

Yoruba Origin

Vibrant wax-printed cotton fabric known for its bold geometric and floral patterns.

Kente

Akan Origin

Handwoven silk and cotton strips featuring intricate geometric designs.

Aso Oke

Yoruba Origin

Hand-loomed cloth traditionally woven by the Yoruba people with metallic threads.

Adire

Yoruba Origin

Indigo-dyed cloth created using resist-dyeing techniques. Each piece is unique.

Lace

European/African Fusion

Delicate embroidered net fabric adapted into African fashion with sequins and beads.

Brocade

Global Heritage

Richly decorative shuttle-woven fabric with raised floral or geometric patterns.

Silk

Asian/African Blend

Luxurious natural silk fabric, often printed with African-inspired motifs.

Velvet

Global Heritage

Soft, plush woven fabric with a dense pile. Deep jewel tones for royal attire.
